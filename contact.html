常见问题 Frequently Asked Questions

如何开始定制我的旅行? How do I start customizing my travel?

您可以通过我们的网站填写定制旅行表单，或直接联系我们的旅行顾问。我们会安排一对一的咨询，了解您的旅行需求和偏好，然后为您设计专属行程。

You can fill out the custom travel form on our website or contact our travel consultants directly. We will arrange a one-on-one consultation to understand your travel needs and preferences, then design a personalized itinerary for you.

定制旅行的价格如何计算? How is the price of custom travel calculated?

定制旅行的价格取决于多个因素，包括目的地、旅行天数、住宿标准、交通方式、用餐安排和特殊活动等。我们会根据您的预算和需求提供不同选择，并提供详细的价格明细。

The price of custom travel depends on multiple factors, including destination, travel days, accommodation standards, transportation methods, dining arrangements, and special activities. We will provide different options based on your budget and needs, along with detailed price breakdowns.

可以修改已确认的行程吗? Can I modify a confirmed itinerary?

是的，我们理解旅行计划可能需要调整。在出发前，您可以与您的旅行顾问沟通修改需求。根据修改的时间和内容，可能会产生一定的变更费用。我们会尽力满足您的需求，同时确保行程的顺利进行。

Yes, we understand that travel plans may need adjustments. Before departure, you can communicate your modification needs with your travel consultant. Depending on the timing and content of the changes, certain modification fees may apply. We will do our best to accommodate your needs while ensuring the smooth progress of your journey.

如何支付旅行费用? How do I pay for my travel?

我们接受多种支付方式，包括银行转账、支付宝、微信支付和信用卡。通常需要支付30%的定金来确认预订，余款在出发前30天支付。对于特殊情况，我们可以提供灵活的支付安排。

We accept various payment methods, including bank transfer, Alipay, WeChat Pay, and credit cards. Typically, a 30% deposit is required to confirm the booking, with the balance due 30 days before departure. For special circumstances, we can provide flexible payment arrangements.
