关于我们 About Us

了解唯伊旅行社的故事、使命和价值观 Learn about Only Truth Travel Agency's story, mission and values

我们的故事 Our Story

唯伊旅行社成立于2010年，由一群热爱旅行、热爱中国文化的旅游专业人士创立。创始人李伊女士曾在国内外知名旅行社工作多年，深感传统旅游模式无法满足现代旅行者对真实、深度体验的需求。

Only Truth Travel Agency was founded in 2010 by a group of travel professionals who are passionate about travel and Chinese culture. Founder Ms. Li Yi worked in well-known travel agencies both domestically and internationally for many years, and deeply felt that traditional tourism models could not meet modern travelers' needs for authentic, in-depth experiences.

我们的名字"唯伊"取自创始人名字，同时"唯"代表专一、专注，"伊"谐音"一"，寓意第一。"唯伊"二字合起来，表达了我们对旅行品质的执着追求和对客户的专一服务理念。英文名"Only Truth"则体现了我们只提供真实体验的核心价值观。

Our name "Wei Yi" comes from the founder's name, while "Wei" represents focus and dedication, and "Yi" sounds like "one", meaning the first. Together, the two characters express our persistent pursuit of travel quality and dedicated service concept for customers. The English name "Only Truth" reflects our core value of providing only authentic experiences.

十多年来，我们专注于为国内高端客户提供中国境内的精品旅行服务，已成功为超过10,000位客户打造了难忘的旅行体验。

For more than a decade, we have focused on providing premium travel services within China for high-end domestic customers, and have successfully created memorable travel experiences for over 10,000 clients.

我们的使命 Our Mission

通过提供真实、深度的旅行体验，让每位客户发现中国之美，感受文化之魅，留下终生难忘的记忆。

To provide authentic, in-depth travel experiences that allow each client to discover the beauty of China, feel the charm of its culture, and create lifelong memories.

    Continuously explore new ways of travel and experiences, bringing surprises and inspiration to clients.
